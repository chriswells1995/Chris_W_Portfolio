About Me

Born and raised in Minnesota, I graduated from the University of Minnesota in Fall of 2018 with a Bachelor of Science in Statistical Science, and minors in Math and Studies in Cinema & Media Culture. After completing an 18-month contract as a Quality Audit Specialist through Robert Half Staffing, and submitting two short films to local festivals, I decided it was time for a career change. I went back to the U of MN in February of 2020 to complete their Full Stack Web Development Coding Bootcamp. I always had an interest in Math, and technical thinking. But I also always loved film and artistic creation. Web development combined these interests, and since graduating with a 4.0 from their break-neck paces, 3-month program, I have been coding non-stop. Now I am continually improving the projects I made in class, working a contract for Driven Business Solutions, and on the lookout for new opportunities. I am always ready to learn, grow, and overcome the next challenge!


Skills include leadership, communication, being quick to learn, and patient to teach.
